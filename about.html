About us

LapManager is designed to give track-day drivers, teams, and enthusiasts complete control over their racing workflow. From planning events and managing detailed car profiles to logging maintenance, costs, and telemetry, every part of your motorsport journey is streamlined in one place.

The platform brings together tools for checklists, reminders, setup management, inventory tracking, and cost analysis so nothing gets overlooked on or off the track.

Whether you’re a solo driver chasing consistency, an enthusiast perfecting your setups through data, or a professional team coordinating multiple cars and members, LapManager ensures your entire operation runs smoothly.

With mobile-first access, cloud-backed storage, and export options for PDF or CSV, you stay organized, informed, and ready to perform - wherever race day takes you.

Our Mission

To empower drivers and teams to maximize performance and safety while simplifying track-day prep. By combining event planning, car management, telemetry analysis, and collaborative tools in a single, intuitive app, we help you focus on what matters most: speed, precision, and enjoyment.

Everything You Need for a Track-Day Workflow

Dashboard & Quick Actions
  • Overview of Upcoming, Today’s, and Recent Events
  • One-click actions: New Event, Add Car, View Logs, Maintenance
  • Team view indicator for collaborative workflows
  • Quick access to subscription status and settings
Car Profiles
  • Add and manage cars with year, make, model, notes, and modification tags
  • Quick actions to edit, manage setups, or jump to maintenance
  • Mobile-ready, tidy layout
Car Setup Management (Enthusiast & Pro)
  • Multiple setups per car (Dry, Wet, Track-specific)
  • Attach setups to events to track history
  • Activate, edit, duplicate, or delete setups
  • Seamless workflow for selecting setups during Track Day planning
Track Day Planning & Logs
  • Plan events with track, date, car, weather, temperature, and notes
  • Event detail view: lap times, fuel usage, tire notes, issues
  • Persist setups and notes per event for accurate history
Checklists & Templates
  • Pre- and post-session templates (default or custom)
  • Assign templates to events and mark items complete per session
  • Create and edit templates for cars, setups, or track conditions
Telemetry Import & Analysis
  • Import RaceChrono CSV files
  • Visualize speed, G-forces, elevation, laps, and session stats
  • Refresh to pull latest telemetry, with validated filters and charts
Maintenance Tracking & Reminders
  • Log maintenance sessions per car with details
  • Schedule reminders for due dates/mileage
  • Export maintenance history to CSV or PDF
Per-Event Cost Tracking
  • Log fuel, entry, travel, accommodation, food, parts, and repairs
  • Instant totals and currency selection
  • Costs feed
Pro-level Global Cost Summary
  • Aggregate event costs with inventory purchases
  • Choose time windows: 3/6/12 months or all time
  • Charts: Pie, Bar, Trend
  • Export to CSV or PDF
Parts & Inventory (Pro)
  • Track parts, tools, quantities, location, price, and purchase date
  • Search and filter efficiently
  • Purchases automatically roll into the Global Cost Summary
Teams & Collaboration (Pro)
  • Create teams, invite members, assign roles (Owner, Editor, Viewer)
  • Switch active team, manage permissions, rename or delete teams
  • Shared context for setups, checklists, and event prep
User Profile & App Settings
  • Profile: first and last name
  • Settings: metric/imperial, PSI/KPA/Bar, dark/light/system theme, PDF size, notifications
  • Local notifications for track reminders and maintenance due dates
