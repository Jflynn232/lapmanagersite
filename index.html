Who is LapManager for?

Track-day drivers
Solo drivers who want a clean event workflow and telemetry insights to improve their lap times and track performance.
Racing enthusiasts
Dedicated enthusiasts iterating on setups and building comprehensive event history to perfect their racing craft.
Team owners & managers
Racing teams needing collaboration tools, inventory management, and detailed cost oversight across multiple vehicles.
Builders & shops
Professional builders and shops managing customer cars or team vehicles with detailed maintenance and setup tracking.

What can LapManager help you do?

Go to the Track Prepared
Checklists and setups ensure nothing is missed. Maintenance logs and reminders keep your car safe and ready for peak performance.
Improve Performance with Data
Import RaceChrono telemetry for lap analysis. Understand speed, G-forces, elevation, and setup impact to find those crucial tenths.
Control Your Costs
Log and analyze per-event costs. Pro-level Global Cost Summary provides insights and exports for budgeting and tax purposes.
Collaborate Like a Team
Shared context, roles, and member management. Standardize prep with templates and setups across your entire racing organization.
Local-First, Cloud-Backed
Fast, responsive UI with structured Supabase data for telemetry, costs, and teams. PDF/CSV exports for sharing and record-keeping.
Built for Mobile & Desktop
Works seamlessly in the paddock or shop. Notifications and reminders keep you on schedule wherever you are.
